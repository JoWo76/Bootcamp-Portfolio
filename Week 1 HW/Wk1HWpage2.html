Facts About Cubism
  Colours, Shapes and Style Date
Analytic Cubism Blue, black, brown, geometric shapes, 2D gradients used 1908-1912
Synthetic Cubism Bright colours, simple shapes, collage used 1912-1914
Artists Pablo Picasso, Juan Gris, Georges Braque,Paul Cezanne, Fernand Leger, Marcel Duchamp
As you can see from the descriptions Analytic and Synthetic Cubism are quite different!
