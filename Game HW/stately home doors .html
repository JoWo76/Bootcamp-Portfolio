You are in the stately home

When you arrive at the stately home you take the stairs up to the front door.

It is a well known building in the area and everyone knows that no one has lived there for years.

You gingerly push open the front door and it opens without any effort.

Once through the door you find yourself in a great hall with large portraits hanging on the walls and three doors each a different colour.
