The Park

You keep walking through the park despite the numerous puddles left by the downpour of rain left this morning.

It really is quite a lovely day. You spot a homeless man snoozing on a bench.

You can hear the chatter of children playing in the nearby play park and love watching the dogs run freely.

In the distance you can see the roof of the derelict stately home that used to be owned by Lord Farquar sparking in the sun.

But you can't help still feeling stressed because you haven't got long to get that story before you don't have a job.


You have three choices. Should you talk to the mums at the park and see if they have any decent gossip worth writing about?

Or should you wake up the homeless man and see if he has any stories to tell.

You can see the derelict stately home in the distance should you walk there and see if there's anything interesting?
